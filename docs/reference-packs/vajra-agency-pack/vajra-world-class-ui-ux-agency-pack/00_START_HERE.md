## Important status

The proposed palette is a strong production-ready starting direction, not a substitute for brand discovery and user testing.

Do not copy the visual identity of Apple, Material, Fluent, Carbon, Primer, GOV.UK, or USWDS. Use their behavioral and systems guidance while giving VAJRA an original identity rooted in evidence, control, routing, verification, and operational trust.
